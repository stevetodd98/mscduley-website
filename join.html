FRA Branch 172

The Fleet Reserve Association represents the interests of Sea Service personnel on Capitol Hill.

Certification of Eligibility:
To join the FRA, you must certify that you meet the eligibility requirements of being a current or prior member of the **Navy, Marine Corps, or Coast Guard**, and that you can produce **proof of service**.

Membership Benefits

  • Legislative advocacy in D.C.
  • Access to FRA national resources
  • Full fellowship at Branch 172

MSC Duley Club

The social heart of our community. Membership in the club provides access to all events, facilities, and the lounge.

Requirements

  • Submission of written application
  • Board of Directors approval
  • Annual dues paid in full
  • Compliance with all House Rules

Why Join?

Membership at MSC Duley is more than just access to a lounge—it's about preserving the bonds of service. Whether it's Sunday Pot Lucks, Shuffleboard tournaments, or community fundraisers, you're among family here.

Note: All MSC Duley members who meet the service requirements are strongly encouraged to join FRA Branch 172 to support our legislative mission.
