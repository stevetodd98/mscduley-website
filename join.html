Membership Requirements

1. Eligibility

Membership is open to veterans and active-duty members of the U.S. Armed Forces, as well as their families and sponsored guests who support our mission.

2. Verification

For veteran membership, please bring a copy of your DD-214 or a valid VA ID card to the club for verification by the Manager or a Board Member.

3. Application & Dues

Pick up a physical application at the bar. Annual dues are required to maintain active status and access to club facilities and gaming.

4. Board Approval

New applications are reviewed by the Board of Directors to ensure the continued integrity and safety of our club community.

Have questions? Reach out to Tiffani Bowmen (Club Manager) or email us at info@mscduley.org.
