Our Mission

The mission of the Military Services Club Duley, as the home of FRA Branch 172, is to foster camaraderie and support by providing a member-driven club where fellowship, advocacy, and community engagement thrive for veterans, active-duty service members, and their families.

📜 Our Heritage

Originally chartered on August 17, 1977, as the FRA Branch 172 Home, our club has stood as a pillar for the Yorktown military community for nearly five decades. In November 2021, we transitioned to become the Military Services Club Duley (MSC Duley), honoring Horace "Ray" Duley, while remaining the proud home port for FRA Branch 172.

May Military Honors

"Remember and Honor"

May 25th marks Memorial Day. Originally known as Decoration Day, it is a day of solemn remembrance for those who died while serving in the U.S. Armed Forces. At MSC Duley, we honor their ultimate sacrifice and the families they left behind.

May is also National Military Appreciation Month, a time to recognize the contributions of all who have served and continue to serve in our nation's uniform.

May Military Remembrance

May 8: V-E Day (Victory in Europe), marking the end of WWII in Europe in 1945.
May 16: Armed Forces Day – honoring those currently serving in all branches.
May 25: Memorial Day (Observed)

May Joint Force History

  • 🎖️ **May 11, 1945:** The aircraft carrier USS Bunker Hill is hit by two Kamikazes off Okinawa.
  • 🛡️ **May 15, 1942:** The Women's Army Auxiliary Corps (WAAC) is established.
  • ⚓ **May 22:** National Maritime Day – recognizing the U.S. Merchant Marine's service.
  • 🎗️ **May 24, 1918:** General Pershing arrives in France during WWI.
