Our Story

The Military Services Club Duley (MSC Duley) was founded on the principles of camaraderie and service. Like the communities of Yorktown and Newport News, we are rooted in history and dedicated to the future of our local veterans.

The Member Experience

Our facility is more than a building; it's a sanctuary for those who served. From our interactive Member Hub where we share our collective history through photos, to our weekly events, we provide a place for veterans to connect.
