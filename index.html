Mission Statement

As the home of FRA Branch 172, the Military Services Club Duley is a welcoming hub for veterans, active-duty service members, and their families from all branches of the U.S. military.

Our mission is to foster camaraderie and support by providing a member-driven club where fellowship, advocacy, and community engagement thrive. We are dedicated to responsibly operating our facilities to advance the welfare of our members and uphold a tradition of honor and mutual respect.

Our Guiding Principles

  • Foster a Community: We provide a dedicated space for members and their families to connect, share experiences, and build lasting friendships.
  • Support Our Members: We stand as a resource for veterans, their families, and their dependents, offering a platform for advocacy and supporting organizations that advance the welfare of the military community.
  • Uphold Our Traditions: We honor the legacy of military service through a commitment to respect, integrity, and the enduring values of our nation's armed forces.
  • Ensure Responsible Stewardship: We will responsibly manage our facilities and finances, including our licensed bar and gaming operations, to ensure the long-term success and stability of the club for all members.
