Supporting Our Shipmates

Your generosity ensures that FRA Branch 172 can continue its century-long mission of Loyalty, Protection, and Service. Whether supporting education or helping a shipmate in need, every contribution counts.

Branch 172 General Fund

Directly supports our local Yorktown operations, community outreach, and the welfare of our branch members.

To Donate: Please contact our Branch Treasurer, Jon Hardy, at our monthly meeting or via the Secretary's office.

FRA Education Foundation

The Foundation awards scholarships to shipmates and their families to help them pursue higher education. Your donation directly impacts the future of the Sea Service family.
