Who We Are

FRA Branch 172, located in Yorktown, Virginia, is a local unit of the Fleet Reserve Association—a congressionally chartered, non-profit organization representing the interests of enlisted Sea Service personnel before the U.S. Congress.

"Our mission is to foster camaraderie and support by providing a member-driven venue where fellowship, advocacy, and community engagement thrive for veterans, active-duty service members, and their families."

Our Cardinal Principles

Loyalty

To God and Country, to the Sea Services, and to our Shipmates.

Protection

Advocating for adequate national defense and the rights of those who serve.

Service

Rendering assistance to our Shipmates and their dependents in good fortune or distress.

Community Impact

Branch 172 isn't just a meeting place; it's a pillar of the Yorktown community. We actively support local NJROTC programs, participate in veteran advocacy, and provide a home-away-from-home for our members at the Military Services Club Duley.

Membership is open to all current and former enlisted members of the **U.S. Navy, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard**. Whether you served for one day or thirty years, you are a Shipmate here.
