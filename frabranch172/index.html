Branch Mission

For 100 years, the Fleet Reserve Association has been the leading voice for the Sea Services. Branch 172 serves the Yorktown community by providing a focused venue for advocacy and fellowship among enlisted shipmates.

Legislative Priorities

Our advocacy protects the hard-earned benefits of those who served in the Navy, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard.

  • Retention of VA benefits and COLA adjustments.
  • Protection of the Military Health System and TRICARE.
  • Proper funding for Sea Service readiness and personnel.

Certification of Eligibility

To join FRA Branch 172, you must certify that you are a current or prior member of the **USN, USMC, or USCG**, and can produce **proof of service**.
