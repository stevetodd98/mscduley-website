Our Mission

Branch 172 of the Fleet Reserve Association (FRA) is dedicated to representing the interests of current and former enlisted members of the **U.S. Navy, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard**.

Since our charter, we have stood as a pillar of advocacy in the Yorktown community, ensuring that the voices of sea service personnel are heard on Capitol Hill and that the bonds of shipmates remain strong long after the uniform is put away.

Membership Eligibility

To join FRA Branch 172, you must certify that you meet the following requirements:

  • Current or prior member of the USN, USMC, or USCG.
  • Able to produce proof of service (DD-214 or ID card).
  • Committed to the principles of Loyalty, Protection, and Service.
