Mission Statement

Branch 172 of the Fleet Reserve Association is dedicated to protecting the rights and benefits of enlisted personnel who have served in the Sea Services. We provide a community of fellowship for those who understand the unique bonds formed at sea.

Legislative Priorities

  • Protecting COLA adjustments for retirees.
  • Ensuring TRICARE remains affordable and accessible.
  • Advocating for full funding of VA disability claims.

Certification of Eligibility

To join FRA Branch 172, you must certify that you are a current or prior member of the **USN, USMC, or USCG**, and can produce **proof of service** (DD-214 or ID Card).
